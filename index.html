How to buy

Be part of history in under a minute

  • Buy BNB & Convert to BSC

    BNB (Binance coin) is available on all major exchanges, then convert your BNB to Binance Smart Chain.

  • Send BSC to Surge address

    Send your BSC to Contract address: 0xE1E1Aa58983F6b8eE8E4eCD206ceA6578F036c21

  • Add Surge as a custom token to your wallet

    Add Contract address: 0xE1E1Aa58983F6b8eE8E4eCD206ceA6578F036c21, Network: Smart Chain, Symbol: SURGE, Decimals: 0.

How to check your surge balance in BNB price

1. Go to the bscScan SURGE contract -> Contract -> Read contract Click Here

2. Paste your wallet address on LINE 7 | getValueOfHoldings

3. Press query - the number displayed is the amount of BNB of the SURGE you are holding, but not has not taken decimals into account.

4. Copy this number and divide the number by 10^18
